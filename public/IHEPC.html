IHEPC

International Hepatitis Club located in MIU is a student activity run by volunteers who aim to provide a Hepatitis C & B Free community, through major awareness campaigns, Screening campaigns and treatment campaigns held by our follow-up team. Our target is to raise awareness about the diseases, avoiding the infection and getting the right treatment. Sponsored by Pharco, we provide screening for all the targeted citizens and the follow up team guides them through the treatment journey.
